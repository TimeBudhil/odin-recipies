Aloo Paratha Orange Chicken Ramen Sushi Green Curry

Ingredients

    Dough
  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • water as needed for kneading (3/4 cup + 2 tablespoons)
    Aloo
  • 3 medium potatoes (~350 grams)
  • 1 green chilli chopped
  • 1/4 teaspoon grated ginger or ginger powder
  • 2-3/4 teaspoon garam masala, to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon kashmiri red chilli powder, to taste
  • 2 tablespoons coriander leaves, finely chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt (to taste)
  • 3/4 taspoon to 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon chaat masala (or 1 tbsp lemon juice or 1/2 tsp amchur)
  • 1 teaspoon kasuri methi dried fenugreek leaves
  • 1/4 teaspoon ajwain (carom seeds), optional
  • 1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds powder, optional
